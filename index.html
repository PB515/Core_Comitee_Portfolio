Case A

Navratri Decoration & Financial Management

Blueprint
Led an 11-volunteer community workforce over a 3-year timeline, translating conceptual designs into physical neighborhood installations, including material sourcing from the Kalupur Tankshal dawn market and full on-site rigging.
Crisis
An inter-family design deadlock emerged: the priest's son wanted a traditional umbrella layout, two core volunteers wanted a modern handicraft setup. All three belonged to the same extended kutumb, so elders refused to intervene to avoid family friction. Operations froze.
Resolution
Ran shuttle diplomacy (individual, private mediations with each party instead of a group confrontation) and engineered a fusion design combining both the traditional umbrella and the modern handicraft elements. Workforce realigned without fracturing family relations.
Governance
Sole accountant for a lean ₹12,000 cap: audited every receipt and maintained the definitive cash ledger (hisab).

Case B

Chaitra Navchandi Havan

Alliance
Coordinated cross-regional scheduling across multiple depopulating historic poles to preserve a 200–500+ year-old heritage tradition, using a sequential macro-schedule across 4 Sundays: Ashapurimata Pole, Upli Seri, Fadiya nu Choktu.
Bottleneck
Multi-stakeholder bookings (Mandap, priests, Samagri) plus mass catering for 500–1,000 people at an event-wide scale of ₹1.5–2L: the legacy seated feast (pangat) caused severe waiting lines and chaotic bottlenecks.
Fix
Shifted to a batch-processed buffet: a strict cap of exactly 100 people per session in the dining zone. Eliminated waiting lines and smoothed the entire service cycle.

06 Full-Stack Deployment · Real-Time Systems

Synergy

A live, production PWA: the shared engagement platform for all 12–13 clubs in the Adani University FMS/Infra Management MBA program, not a single-club toy.

Next.js · Supabase (RLS + Realtime) · Vercel, live in production
What it is

One engagement system, every club

Mechanic
Club participation (meetings, expert sessions, volunteer tasks) earns "Joules" via QR check-in and live quizzes, with standing tiers (Ember → Volt → Current → Plasma) and a universal leaderboard ranking every student, not just a top 10.
Live Round
A host-paced, Kahoot-style real-time quiz mode with pooled team scoring, negative marking, and time-based bonus: built after a professor asked for exactly this during a live review.
Access control
Three-tier RBAC (Professor / Committee Member / Student) enforced with Postgres Row-Level Security, not just hidden UI. A Committee Member is scoped to their own club at the database layer.
Proof, not claims

Found under pressure, fixed under pressure

Security
A self-run adversarial security retrospective found a live, exploitable vulnerability: an internal secret callable by any anonymous visitor. Closed it in production before it could be abused.
Live testing
Real-device testing (not just a passing build) caught a system-wide bug where reward-crediting had silently failed for every student since launch. Found, fixed, and re-verified directly against production.
Today
Demoed live with real students on 17 July 2026: a real-device Live Round across separate phones and a laptop host. That session surfaced five more real bugs, all fixed the same day.
